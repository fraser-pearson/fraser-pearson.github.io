Neuroplasticity

Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people.

Neuroplasticity is the brain's ability to adapt and change to situations. Forming new connections and pathways changing how our neurons travel through our brain. The benefits of understanding neuroplasticity can be improved learning ability, and increase weakened cognitive abilities like memory.

Discuss how you might engage with the principles of neuroplasticity for your benefit.

To engage with neuroplasticity you can perform mind exercises to improve your neuroplasticity. Getting a good amount of sleep also helps. Making sure your mental health is in order will also improve your neuroplasticity

What are some of the ways to increase your neuroplasticity?

To increase your neuroplasticity you can do all sorts of things like playing games, exercise, travel and make some art. As long as the task relaxes your brain, basically anything can be helpful.

Research Growth Mindset

Discuss what it is and why it is relevant

Having a Growth Mindset is the idea of constantly improving yourself and not sagnating. Compared to a fixed mindset you will push through failure to see success later one, you will put effort in learning new skills and you take feedback to improve yourself. It's relevant as when learning anything you should have a good growth mindset, it's ok to fail or struggle to understand something you are doing this to improve yourself for the future.

In this exploration, did anything surprise you? Change for you?

I found researching the growth mindset quite interesting although I don't believe anything changed for me. I already had a growth mindset so I have been always thinking ahead knowing that my learning will be helpful in the future and it will take effort to achieve this goal.

How will you integrate a growth mindset into your learning journey?

On my learning journey I will think about a growth mindset if I come across a wall. I know it may take effort to overcome it but it will be worth it for the future. I know this learning journey wont be a walk in the park and I will have to be motivated to complete it.
